Corporate Profile

Prinpack Supplies is a leading paper indenting agent in Malaysia, founded by Mr. K.K.Cheng (Managing Director) and established since 1983. We are paper mill's representative specialise in selling wide range of paper and board for Commercial, Industrial, Speciality and Converting Usage used by our customers across a broad spectrum of applications.


Foothold by our strong customer network and our global link to suppliers and paper mills, we delivered our services across 38 years of history and we will consistently create value to serve our customers’ business growth.


Prinpack supplies a broad range of paper products and prepare to serve the needs of the different markets sectors we operate in. We market some products under exclusive or non-exclusive distribution  arrangement.
